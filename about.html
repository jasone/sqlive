SkipLabs

SkipLabs is a small and dynamic company based in NYC. All of our code is written in a proprietary fork of Skip (http://skiplang.com). Skip is a programming language originally developed by Facebook (open-sourced in 2018). Skip's unique features, such as the tracking of side effects to provide caching with reactive invalidation, ergonomic and safe parallelism, and efficient garbage collection makes it a perfect tool to write innovative software. In 2019, SkipLabs forked the open-source version to create a proprietary version supporting persistence.
